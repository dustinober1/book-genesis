## 1. Purpose

This ledger tracks major reveals, their first hints, reinforcements, reversals, and payoffs.

Use this document to make sure the final reveals of *Someone Who Leaves* feel inevitable in retrospect but not obvious too early.

---

## 2. Reveal Rules

Every major reveal must have:

- a first hint
- at least one reinforcement
- a false or innocent explanation
- a darker interpretation
- a payoff
- an emotional consequence

A reveal should change both plot understanding and emotional stakes.

## 5. Reveal Quality Checklist

Before approving a reveal, answer:

1. Was it planted early enough?
2. Does it have an innocent explanation?
3. Does it change the reader's understanding of earlier scenes?
4. Does it affect Norah emotionally?
5. Does it affect Theo emotionally?
6. Does it change the institution's position?
7. Does it avoid feeling like exposition?
8. Does it create a new consequence?
