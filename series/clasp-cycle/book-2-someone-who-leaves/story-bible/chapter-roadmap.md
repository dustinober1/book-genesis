# Someone Who Leaves — Chapter Roadmap

Series: Clasp Cycle  
Book: Someone Who Leaves  
Author Brand: Nessa Keane  
Status: Roadmap Scaffold / Pre-Draft  
Last Updated: 2026-06-07

---

## 1. Purpose

This roadmap will become the chapter-by-chapter control map for *Someone Who Leaves*.

It should be built before drafting Chapter 1.

Each chapter must have a clear purpose, escalation, emotional movement, Harbor movement, and ending turn.

No chapter should exist only to explain or transition.

---

## 2. Roadmap Rules

Each chapter must answer:

1. What changes permanently?
2. What does Norah want?
3. What does Theo want?
4. What does Harbor gain?
5. What does the institution document or believe?
6. What evidence is created, revealed, or reframed?
7. What pressure pulls the reader forward?
