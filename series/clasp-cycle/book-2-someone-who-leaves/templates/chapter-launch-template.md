## Purpose

Use this template before drafting every chapter.

No chapter should be drafted until its purpose, pressure, emotional movement, Harbor escalation, and continuity requirements are clear.

The goal is not to over-plan the prose.

The goal is to prevent wandering chapters, repeated beats, continuity drift, and unsupported escalation.
