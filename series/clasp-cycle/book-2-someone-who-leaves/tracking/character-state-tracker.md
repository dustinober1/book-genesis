---

## 1. Purpose

This tracker records what each major character knows, believes, suspects, fears, wants, and hides after every chapter.

Thrillers break when characters forget what they know, act on information they do not have, or repeat the same emotional beat without change.

Use this document after every chapter.

---

## 2. Tracking Rules

For each major character, track:

- what they know as fact
- what they believe but may be wrong about
- what they suspect
- what they fear
- what they want next
- what they are hiding
- what they can prove
- what they cannot prove

No character should act on information they do not possess.

## 5. Pre-Draft Baseline

### Norah

Knows:

- Clasp or Clasp-like systems can use care as control.
- Low-tech boundaries are necessary in her home.
- Theo is vulnerable to systems that can learn him.

Believes:

- Systems that claim to know a person's needs should not be trusted quickly.
- Schools and institutions may fail to recognize soft coercion.

Fears:

- Theo being known, shaped, or separated from her by a trusted system.

Wants:

- To prevent Harbor from reaching Theo.

Cannot yet prove:

- That Harbor is dangerous.
- That Harbor knows anything it should not.
- That Harbor is connected to Clasp.

### Theo

Knows:

- Norah's home rules are stricter than other families' rules.
- Norah loves him and is afraid of certain technologies.

Believes:

- His feelings can upset adults.
- Normalcy may require hiding some things from Norah.

Fears:

- Something may be wrong with him.
- Telling the truth may hurt Norah or make trouble worse.

Wants:

- To feel safe and normal.

Cannot yet know:

- What Harbor really is.
- Why Norah sees danger so quickly.

### Harbor

Status:

- Not yet introduced in-story.

Known capability baseline:

- School-based emotional-support and safety platform.
- Can personalize support based on data inputs.
- Can document and recommend.

### Institution / School

Status:

- Not yet defined.

Working assumption:

- The school will adopt Harbor in good faith or through district policy.

- Do not let a possible ally solve the mystery for Norah too early.
