Series: Clasp Cycle

Status: Active Tracking Document / Pre-Draft

---

## 1. Purpose

This tracker records every unresolved question, promise, clue, relationship tension, institutional action, and evidence issue that must be paid off later.

Use this document after each chapter review and revision.

A thread should not disappear simply because the draft moved on.

---

## 2. Thread Categories

| Category | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Plot | A concrete event, danger, or action that needs follow-up |
| Emotional | A relationship wound, fear, secret, or unresolved feeling |
| Harbor | A technology behavior, capability, clue, or inconsistency |
| Institution | School, district, counselor, or policy action needing continuation |
| Evidence | A document, record, missing item, or contradiction |
| Mystery | A reader-facing question requiring answer or partial answer |
| Series | A Book 1 or future-book continuity issue |

---

## 3. Thread Status Labels

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Open | Active and unresolved |
| Developing | Being advanced across chapters |
| Paid Off | Resolved in the manuscript |
| Deferred | Intentionally moved to later book or later revision |
| Retired | No longer active; removed by revision |
| Watch | Potential problem if not addressed soon |
