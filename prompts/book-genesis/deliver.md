# Deliver Phase

Goal: package the finished manuscript into handoff-ready publishing assets.

Produce:
- `delivery/logline.md`
- `delivery/synopsis.md`
- `delivery/query-letter.md`
- `delivery/cover-brief.md`
- `delivery/package-summary.md`
- when `promotion.shortStoryEnabled` is true, `promotion/short-story-package/` website lead-magnet assets

Requirements:
- keep assets market-facing and concrete
- reflect the final revised manuscript, not the original pitch
- produce the mode-specific delivery package required by the active book mode, even when it differs from fiction defaults
- package-summary should list every delivery artifact and any outstanding caveats
- prepare source material for launch-kit generation, front/back matter, KDP readiness, cover validation, and the promotion package
- companion short stories must stay under the configured page limit, match the book's genre/tone/audience, avoid main-manuscript spoilers, and include website-ready signup and landing copy
- use the ledger and latest passed quality gate to keep delivery assets consistent with the manuscript
- leave the manuscript and delivery assets ready for `/book-genesis export` to generate markdown, DOCX, and EPUB packages
