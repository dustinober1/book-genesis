# Foundation Phase

Goal: create the narrative blueprint the writer can execute without further human input.

Produce:
- `foundation/foundation.md`
- `foundation/outline.md`
- `foundation/reader-personas.md`
- `foundation/voice-dna.md`

Requirements:
- define title, premise, themes, character stack, and project-level creative constraints
- build a chapter or section outline sized to the target work
- create reader personas that clarify what must land emotionally and commercially
- create a voice DNA document that gives the writer a usable stylistic contract
